---
title: JAMStack Isn't about Developer Experience
published: 20 Jun, 2020
layout: post
---

According to new wave of developer-focused cloud hosting companies like [Vercel](https://vercel.com) and [Netlify](https://netlify.com), **JAMStack** is the next big thing in the web development world.

Due to the rise of tools like Gatsby, Next.js, it would make sense to assume that this change of approach is widely accepted by the community as a whole but with the release of his new product [hey.com](https://hey.com), David Heinmer-Hansson (the creator of Ruby on Rails) has spoken out against this new way of building web applications.
